FAQ

Common questions

How does LUMA handle messages it can't answer?
LUMA uses confidence scoring on every response. When it detects it isn't confident enough, it instantly acknowledges the customer and creates a ticket in your escalation queue. The bot stays silent on that conversation until you resolve it — no awkward double-replies.
What languages does LUMA support?
Arabic (Modern Standard and Egyptian dialect), English, and Franco Arabic (the Arabic-in-Latin-letters style common across the Middle East). LUMA auto-detects the language and responds in kind — no configuration needed.
How does the "80% auto-resolved" claim work?
LUMA has a pre-filter layer that runs before the AI. It handles greetings, thank-yous, duplicate messages, emoji-only messages, order tracking queries, and more — all with zero AI cost. These typically make up 80% of all incoming messages. Only complex questions reach the AI engine.
Does LUMA know about my products?
Yes. LUMA syncs your entire Shopify product catalog automatically every 6 hours. When a customer asks about a product, LUMA searches your inventory in real-time and provides accurate stock, pricing, and product details.
Is my customer data safe?
Absolutely. LUMA uses a secure architecture that keeps each store's data completely separate — your store's data is completely separated. We verify webhook signatures (industry-standard security verification), enforce Shopify's GDPR compliance, and all API communications use HTTPS.
Can I customize the bot's responses?
Yes. You get a full template library where you can create custom auto-reply rules per message category. You can also adjust the bot's greeting style, set custom escalation rules, and pause the bot at any time for manual takeover.
